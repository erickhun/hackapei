FAQ

Do I have to be a hardcore coder?
Nope — but you do need to be building your own project. Designers, hardware hackers, indie founders, weekend tinkerers all welcome. If you just want to watch the first time, that's fine too.
Where exactly do you meet?
We find a calm space to focus — usually a co-working spot around Da'an / Xinyi or Ximen, a short walk from the MRT, where everyone grabs a day pass so we can actually be productive. It rotates week to week, so we DM you the exact pin after you sign up.
How much does it cost?
Free. You cover your own day pass, coffee and lunch.
Can I just come and watch the first time?
Of course. Plenty of people lurk the first week, see what others are building, and jump in when they're ready.
How do I actually get in?
Sign up above. It's invite-first and we grow slow, stable & steady — we'll reach out on Telegram with the details.
